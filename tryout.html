KATIPUNAN PH TRYOUTS


We want to maintain a healthy and toxic-free environment in the clan,
while also maintaining a competitive and positive atmosphere

1. Willing to undergo Tryouts set by admins
2. Non-toxic player who is at least 16 years old or above
3. Player level must be at maximum and with 1k + matches
4. Must have a discord account
5. Not a clan hopper, with pleasing personality
6. Willing to follow the Clan Rules
(which includes being active in Group chat,
Ingame, and in Clan scrimmages and Tournaments)
7. Also inform the admins if you want a solo tryout or squad tryout
